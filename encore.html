One more time

The Seven Plays in English Verse by Sophocles

CR.

Thine is a shallow judgement, if thou thinkest Self-will without true thought can bring thee gain.

OED.

Thine is a shallow judgement, if thou thinkest Thou canst abuse thy kinsman and be free.

CR.

A rightful sentence. But I fain would learn What wrong is that you speak of?

OED.

Tell me this; Didst thou, or not, urge me to send and bring The reverend-seeming prophet?
